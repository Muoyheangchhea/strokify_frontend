F.A.S.T.

F.A.S.T. test is an easy way to detect some sudden signs and symptoms of a stroke and prompt immediate actions:

Face Drooping

Does one side of the face droop? Ask the person to smile. Is that face symmetrical?

Arm Weakness

Is one arm weak or numb? Ask the person to raise both arms. Does one arm drift downward?

Speech Difficulty

Is speech slurred, are they unable to speak or understand? Ask the person to repeat a simple sentence.

Time To CALL

Go to the hospital immediately!
